Special Olympics World Games: Los Angeles 2015

The Special Olympics officially opened Saturday in a ceremony at the Los Angeles Coliseum hosted by none other than Michelle Obama. Over the next two weeks, over 6,000 athletes from 2,000 different countries will compete across a range of competitions, some of which will be taking place here in Westwood. This page will serve all your news needs regarding this summer's Special Olympics as the Daily Bruin reports live from every event. (Daniel Alcazar/Daily Bruin Senior Staff)
